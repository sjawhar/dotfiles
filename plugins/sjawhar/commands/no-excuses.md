# No Excuses

Do not justify; remediate.

1. Reject excuse patterns (pre-existing, known bug, not our fault, wording debate, risky shortcut).
2. Restate the user's goal.
3. Provide and execute a remediation path now.
4. Verify user-observable impact with evidence.
