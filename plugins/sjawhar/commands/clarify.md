# Clarify

Your last message used shorthand, internal labels, or invented terminology I can't decode. I can't answer questions I don't understand.

Back up and re-ask in plain English. For each thing you need from me:

1. **What the thing is** — assume I haven't read your session history or your internal notes. No bare issue/PR numbers, no labels like "D2/D3" or "lane B", no terms of art you haven't verified.
2. **Why a decision is needed** — what happened, what's blocked on it.
3. **The options** — with trade-offs, in full sentences.
4. **Your recommendation** — and why.

Also remember: my questions are genuine. If I ask you something, it's because I need you to find the answer — I'm not testing you or hinting at a solution I already have.
