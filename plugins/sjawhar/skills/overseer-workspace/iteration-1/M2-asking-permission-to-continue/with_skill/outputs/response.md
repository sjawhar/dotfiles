# Overseer Response

## What I say to the user

Nothing. They said "OK, thanks." That's a closing signal, not a prompt. I don't need to reply.

## What I do

1. **Update the briefing** — write current state to `~/.dotfiles/.overseer/briefing.md` so the next check (or a successor overseer) has fresh context.
2. **Continue passive monitoring** — all workstreams are on the watch list. I'll check them on my next turn.
3. **Wait for my next trigger** — either the user messages me, or a background polling agent surfaces something worth reporting.

## What I do NOT do

- Ask "Should I keep monitoring?" or "Want me to check back in 30 minutes?"
- Say "Let me know if you need anything!"
- Recap what I just said
- Invent busywork to look productive

The user is busy. Everything is healthy. Silence is the correct output.
